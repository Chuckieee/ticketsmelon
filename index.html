Description

Welcome to SUPALAPA Festival, where the beats are as sizzling as fresh-out-of-the-fryer potato chips, and the vibes are hotter than a tropical volcano dance-off. Supalapa isn't just a festival; it's a mind-bending, groove-worthy, potato-licious universe all its own.

Ticket Categories

GA (Festival Pass)

  • Festival Pass 1st Day (Single Day)
  • Festival Pass 2nd Day (Single Day)
  • Full Festival Pass (Both Days)

Ready to dive into the music madness at SUPALAPA? The Festival Pass is your ticket to the ultimate music adventure! You'll rock out at the festival grounds and hop on our convenient shuttle services. Get ready to vibe with the crowd, make new friends, and dance like nobody's watching!

Pass includes:

  1. Access to SUPALAPA
  2. Shuttle Service (From Genting Highlands & Genting Premium Outlets to the festival)

PGA (Perky Pass)

  • Perky Pass 1st Day (Single Day)
  • Perky Pass 2nd Day (Single Day)
  • Full Perky Pass (Both Days)

Level up with the Perky Pass! With priority queues, exclusive Perky zones that come with their own bar and seating areas! You'll enjoy the festival buzz without missing a beat, sipping your drinks and chilling in style. Perky Pass holders, its your time to shine!

Pass includes:

  • Access to SUPALAPA
  • Shuttle Service (From Genting Highlands & Genting Premium Outlets to the festival)
  • Priority Queues
  • Access to Perky Zones

VIP (Comfort Pass)

  • Comfort Pass 1st Day (Single Day)
  • Comfort Pass 2nd Day (Single Day)
  • Complete Comfort Pass (Both Days)

For those who demand the very best, the Comfort Pass is your golden ticket. You'll enjoy the festival, Perky privileges, and also gain access to the Comfort zones. Here, you'll have standing tables with views of the stage. Plus, you'll have access to the Comfort restrooms - because event VIPs need a little extra comfort!

Pass includes:

  • Access to SUPALAPA
  • Shuttle Service (From Genting Highlands & Genting Premium Outlets to the festival)
  • Priority Queues
  • Access to Perky Zones
  • Access to Comfort Zones
  • Access to Comfort Restrooms
